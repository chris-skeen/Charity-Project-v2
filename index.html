Spread Music

At Spread Music we aim to increase the amount of access to music in the world! We travel all over the world to less fortunate countries and pay for musical equipment. We also teach them how to operate the musical insturments and inspire people to play music!
We also teach others how to teach music! If you come in with some experience in the musical field we can teach you in no time. If you would like to sign up to teach others please navigate to the bottom of the page.
